Different Neighborhoods

Jennifer Hodge Updated on February 21, 2023 Chicago, Illinois, USA

No matter your reason for coming here, whether you are getting away for the day
or you are in the process of re-locating for work, Chicago has a variety of great
trendy Neighborhoods to suit all taste and lifestyles.






Here is a cool video to give you some insight to some of
the great neighborhoods that are my favs!











River North

My friend lives in this area! :)

Located in downtown Chicago is a trendy district with modern
design architecture just a few blocks away from the cities
premiere shopping district Michigan Avenue. It has coffee
shops, clothing, restaurants of all varieties and of course,
my friend lives there!




Streeterville

This is a Target I like to go to in
Streeterville off the corner of E.
Illinois Street!

If you are out and about in downtown Chicago and want to find
some fun activities, Streeterville has something for you! I like
to go there because of all the architecture, such as the the
Tribune Tower. The Navy Pier is also another place my friend
and I, like to venture to because of all the restaurants, the view
and of course all the different places to walk!
( We are some loooong walkers! LOL)

Gold Coast

I walk this area all the time!

The Gold Coast neighborhood has been on of the wealthiest and luxurious
places to live in downtown Chicago for decades. High end clothing stores such as Prada, Fendi and others
line the streets and don't be surprised if you see a Rolls Royce or Mercedes-Benz driving down the street.
